In 2005, with immense courage and the collective savings of the family, the first MR Restaurant opened its doors on No.72 Kamaraj road, Thoothukudi. It was a modest space with just 42 tables, but it was built on an immovable foundation: authentic recipes, generational wisdom, and respect for every single guest. The early days were powered by sheer passion. Mr.Mathi would be at the market at dawn, selecting the freshest produce and spices. The entire family would grind masalas by hand, slow-cook gravies for hours, and personally welcome each customer. Word spread quickly. We weren't just a restaurant; we were an extension of that warm, familial kitchen where everyone was treated like family.

From Our Family Hearth to Your Table: Chef & Owner prakash

The story of MR Restaurant is, in essence, the story of Chef prakash's family. What began as cherished recipes cooked for lively family gatherings has now blossomed into this beloved dining destination. As the founder and heart of the kitchen, Chef prakash is personally involved in every detail—from selecting the day's freshest produce at the market to perfecting the slow-cooked richness of our iconic [Signature Dish, e.g., "Family Recipe Butter Chicken"]. There are no shortcuts here, only the love and patience that home cooking demands, scaled for our extended family—you, our guests.

Our Mission

To nourish our community with the authentic, vibrant flavors of India, crafted with reverence for tradition and a commitment to exceptional quality. We are dedicated to creating more than just a meal—we create a warm, memorable experience where every guest feels like family and leaves with a deeper appreciation for India's rich culinary heritage.

Our Core Values

We believe food must feed the soul. We achieve this by honoring generations-old recipes, toasting and blending our own spices daily, and cooking with the utmost care and intention. Authenticity is our cornerstone, never compromised.
